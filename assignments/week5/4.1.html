Rolex Submariner

The Rolex Submariner is an iconic diver's watch known for its robustness, precision, and timeless design. It's water-resistant up to 300 meters and features a unidirectional rotatable bezel for tracking dive time.

Specifications

Price: Prices can vary depending on the specific model and market conditions, but generally, they range from $8,000 to $40,000+.

Brand: Rolex

Model No.: Various models available, such as 114060 (No Date) and 116610 (Date)

Omega

The Omega Speedmaster Professional, often referred to as the Moonwatch, has a legendary status as the first watch worn on the moon during the Apollo 11 mission. It boasts a durable construction and a chronograph function, making it a favorite among astronauts and watch enthusiasts alike.

Specifications

Price:Prices typically start around $3,000 and can go up to $10,000 or more for special editions or vintage pieces.

Brand: Omega

Model No.: Various models available, including the classic 311.30.42.30.01.005

Patek Philippe Nautilus

The Patek Philippe Nautilus is a luxury sports watch celebrated for its distinctive porthole-inspired design and high-end craftsmanship. It combines elegance with durability, making it suitable for both formal occasions and daily wear.

Specifications

Price:Prices for the Nautilus vary greatly depending on materials and complications, with entry-level models starting around $30,000 and reaching well into six figures for complicated versions in precious metals.

Brand:Patek Philippe

Model No.:Various models available, such as 5711/1A-010
